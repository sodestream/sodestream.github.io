Tooling

The backbone of our tooling is the ietfdata library, which comprises an easy to use Python interface to the publicly available IETF data. On top of it we have an additional set of tools which provide further functionalities such as preprocessing the email texts or building a social graph of participants. To learn more about the IETF and all available tooling we recommend you have a look at our quickstart tutorial.

The sodestream project is funded by the UK Engineering and Physical Sciences Research Council, under grants EP/S033564/1 and EP/S036075/1.
