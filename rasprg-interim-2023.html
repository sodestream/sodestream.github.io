RASP interim meeting (IETF116-117)

by Mladen Karan • Friday 2 June 2023 • Permalink

The interim meeting of the Research and Analysis of Standard-Setting Processes Proposed Research Group was held on 16 May 2023. Several members of the sodestream team participated ( (Ignacio Castro, Stephen McQuistin, and Colin Perkins) and were involved in the discussion. For more info about the discussion you can have a look at the meeting agenda, minutes, and materials.

The sodestream project is funded by the UK Engineering and Physical Sciences Research Council, under grants EP/S033564/1 and EP/S036075/1.
