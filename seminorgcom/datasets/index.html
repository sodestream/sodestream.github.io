IETF data

In scope of the sodestream project we have been working with data from the Internet Engineering Task Force email archive and can provide additional support / tooling for this dataset: preprocessed email text, participant interaction graph, and a python library to fetch additional metadata. For more information see our publications on internet standard deployment and on social interactions in the mailing lists, which both explore this data, or contact us by email.
